Act VI — Two futures, next July
● With Threshold

A duty operator at Alectra opens this map at 6am, sees Margaret's neighbourhood flagged critical, and gets a crew out by 9. Someone knocks on her door. She stays cool. She eats that week.

● Without it

The platform doesn't exist. Nobody's watching. The threshold gets crossed — and by the time anyone responds, it's day three.

Four students from Seneca. Real ML, real design, real data — built in weeks. Not because we had to. Because the most vulnerable people in this city deserve someone in their corner before the crisis, not after.
